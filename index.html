What is a PingPong Token?

PingPong Token represents a right to receive a part of PingPong balls, which could be easily exchanged for BTC. Moreover, PingPongers community is engaged in voting over following matters: the product development, the companyâ€™s marketing strategy, the product design. Payout Structure:

By the end of each fiscal year, the company exchanges 50% of its profit for BTC which are used to purchase PingPong balls (P-balls).Those P-balls are distributed proportionally among PingPongers depending on token quantity. PingPongers are able to exchange their P-balls for BTC as well.
